ACM-W Teaching

The purpose of ACM-W in this event is to work with the Girl Scouts of Orange County and teach girls about the world of engineering. The goal of ACM-W is to get more girls interested in computer science by exposing them to programming at a young age.

During the Spring 2016 semester, the ACM-W Chapter at CSUF and the Girl Scouts of Orange County participated in a mentorship program called Creative Coding. With sponsorship from IgniteCS at Google, they explored Turtle Graphics and Ozobots. YouTube video
